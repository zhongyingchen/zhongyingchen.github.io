Zhongying Chen

     Hong Kong, 2024

I am an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Southeast University, Nanjing, China.

I received a B.E. degree in energy engineering from Xi’an Jiaotong University in 2017 and an M.Sc. degree in mechanical engineering from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2018.
I was a Ph.D. student in mechanical engineering at HKUST during 2018–2020. I went to City University of Hong Kong (CityU) in 2020 and received a Ph.D degree in Computer Science from CityU in 2023, where I was so fortunate to be advised by Prof. Jun Wang (Member of Academia Europaea, IEEE Life Fellow, IAPR Fellow, Dean of the School of Data Science). I joined Southeast University in 2024.

My research interests include optimization methods, neural networks, computational intelligence, and smart energy systems.

We are looking for postgraduate and undergraduate students to join our group at Southeast University. Please email me the CV and transcript if you are interested.

Hongzong Li is my group mate at CityU. He will graduate in 2024 and is currently in the job market. Feel free to contact him if you are interested in his profile.
